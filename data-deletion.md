# Data Deletion for GeoPDF Maps

GeoPDF Maps does not require an account and does not store user data on our own server.

## Delete Saved Map Data

GeoPDF Maps stores selected PDF maps and map thumbnails locally on your device so that you can reopen saved maps without selecting the PDF again.

To delete saved map data:

1. Open GeoPDF Maps.
2. Go to the map tile screen.
3. Open the menu on the saved map tile.
4. Select **Remove tile**.

This removes the saved PDF map and related thumbnail from the app's local storage.

You can also delete all locally stored app data by uninstalling GeoPDF Maps from your device.

## Data Deletion Requests

GeoPDF Maps does not have user accounts and does not maintain a server-side user profile for deletion.

If you need help with deleting locally stored app data, or if you have a privacy-related deletion request, contact:

geokyma@gmail.com

Please include:

- The app name: GeoPDF Maps
- A short description of the data deletion request
- The device type if your request relates to local app storage

## Data That May Be Kept by Third Parties

GeoPDF Maps uses third-party services for ads, online basemaps, and Google Play features.

These services may process data according to their own privacy and retention policies:

- Google AdMob and Google Play services
- Mapbox

To manage or delete data held by those services, use the privacy controls provided by Google and Mapbox.

Google privacy controls:
https://myaccount.google.com/privacy

Mapbox privacy policy:
https://www.mapbox.com/legal/privacy

## Retention

Saved PDF maps and related local files remain on your device until you remove the map tile, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app.

GeoPDF Maps does not keep a server-side copy of your saved PDF maps.
