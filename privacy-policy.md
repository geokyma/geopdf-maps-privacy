# Privacy Policy for GeoPDF Maps

Effective date: May 26, 2026

GeoPDF Maps is an offline GeoPDF map viewer with live GPS support. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is handled, and which third-party services are used.

## Information We Collect

GeoPDF Maps does not require an account and does not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or contact information.

The app may access or use the following information on your device:

- Location data: used to show your current position and direction on the selected GeoPDF map.
- PDF files selected by you: used to display and georeference your map.
- App usage data related to ads and app functionality: processed by third-party services such as Google AdMob and Mapbox as described below.

## Location Data

GeoPDF Maps uses your device location to display your position on the map and to provide live GPS navigation features.

Location data is used on your device for map display. GeoPDF Maps does not send your selected PDF maps or your location history to our own server.

The app may request foreground location permission. Location is used while the app is open and the map/GPS features are active.

## PDF Maps and Local Storage

When you select a PDF map, GeoPDF Maps stores a local copy of the selected file and related map tile information on your device so that the map can be reopened without selecting the PDF again.

These files are stored locally inside the app's private storage area. They are not uploaded to our server.

You can remove saved maps from within the app by deleting the relevant map tile.

## Basemaps and Mapbox

GeoPDF Maps can display optional online basemaps, including topographic and satellite basemaps. These basemaps are provided through Mapbox.

When an online basemap is enabled, Mapbox may receive technical information required to serve map tiles, such as your device's IP address, requested map tile locations, app requests, and related usage information.

GeoPDF Maps caches some basemap tiles on your device to reduce repeated network requests and improve performance.

Mapbox's privacy policy is available at:
https://www.mapbox.com/legal/privacy

## Advertising and Google AdMob

GeoPDF Maps uses Google AdMob to show ads, including banner ads and rewarded ads.

Rewarded ads may be used to unlock temporary app features, such as additional map tiles or online basemap access for a limited time.

Google AdMob may collect and process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, ad interactions, diagnostics, and other data needed to provide, measure, and improve ads.

Google's privacy policy is available at:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google's information about ads is available at:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

## In-App Review

GeoPDF Maps may use Google Play's in-app review feature to ask whether you would like to leave a review after using the app several times. Review submission is handled by Google Play.

## Internet Access

GeoPDF Maps can work with saved GeoPDF maps without an internet connection. Internet access is used for optional online basemaps, ads, and related third-party services.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

GeoPDF Maps may share data with third-party service providers only as needed for app functionality, including:

- Google AdMob for advertising.
- Google Play services for in-app review and app platform functionality.
- Mapbox for online basemap tiles.

These third-party services process data according to their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

GeoPDF Maps is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

## Data Retention

Saved PDF maps and related local files remain on your device until you remove them from the app or uninstall the app.

Third-party services such as Google AdMob, Google Play, and Mapbox may retain data according to their own retention policies.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Deny or revoke location permission in your device settings.
- Use saved GeoPDF maps without enabling online basemaps.
- Remove saved map tiles from within the app.
- Reset or limit your device advertising ID using your Android privacy settings.
- Uninstall the app to remove locally stored app data.

## Security

GeoPDF Maps stores selected maps in the app's private storage area on your device. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, but we use platform-provided storage and permissions to protect local app data.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted by updating the effective date and policy text.
